## 9. HIPAA-Enabled Features

For tenants operating in environments subject to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), Snap4Knack offers an optional HIPAA Mode. **HIPAA Mode is not enabled by default.** Tenants who process Protected Health Information (PHI) must:

1. **Contact Fine Mountain Consulting LLC** to discuss executing a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) before enabling HIPAA Mode or using the service in any HIPAA-covered context
2. Enable HIPAA Mode within tenant settings, which activates:
   - Automated data loss prevention (DLP) scanning of all incoming submissions
   - PHI detected in screenshots, descriptions, and comments is flagged and held in a staging state pending review or redaction
   - Email notification content is stripped of submission details when PHI has been flagged
   - A 7-year data retention minimum is enforced for submissions associated with HIPAA-covered workflows
3. **Screen recording is disabled when HIPAA Mode is active**

**Fine Mountain Consulting LLC does not warrant that HIPAA Mode alone satisfies all HIPAA obligations.** Covered Entities and Business Associates remain responsible for their own compliance programs, policies, and controls. Use of Snap4Knack without a signed BAA in a covered context may constitute a HIPAA violation for which the tenant is solely responsible.

## 10. Activity and Audit Logging

Snap4Knack maintains an immutable audit trail for each submission that records:

- All status changes (e.g., Open → In Progress → Resolved)
- All priority changes

This activity log is visible to authorized team members and tenant administrators. Audit records cannot be edited or deleted by any user. Retention of audit records follows the tenant's configured data retention policy (or the HIPAA minimum if applicable).

## 11. Data Retention and Deletion

Tenants may configure a data retention window within platform settings. After the configured retention period expires:

For HIPAA-enabled tenants, a minimum 7-year retention period is enforced regardless of tenant settings.

Upon account termination, tenant data is retained for 30 days to allow export, after which it is permanently deleted.
